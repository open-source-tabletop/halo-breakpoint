### Spartans

| Model             | Cmd | Mov | Skl | Def | Tgh | HP  | Special Rules        | Points |
| :---------------- | :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: | :------------------- | :----- |
| Spartan II / IV   |  6  |  7" |  2+ |  2+ |  6  |  4  | Officer, Shields     | 65     |
| Spartan III       |  5  |  7" |  2+ |  3+ |  5  |  3  | Active Camo, Officer | 50     |

#### Configuration

A Spartan unit consists of 1 Spartan II, III, or IV.

#### Equipment

A Spartan is equipped with an MA5 Assault Rifle, an M6 Pistol, M9 Grenades, and Melee Strike (6). A Spartan may replace its MA5 Assault Rifle with any of the following:

- M6 Pistol
- M90 Shotgun
- BR55 Service Rifle
- M7 SMG
- M41 Rocket Launcher
- SRS99 Sniper Rifle

A Spartan may instead replace both its MA5 Assault Rifle and M6 Pistol with Twin M7 SMGs.

A Spartan II or Spartan IV may choose one of the following items:

- Active Camouflage
- Overshield
- Jet Pack

| ODST              |  4  |  6" |  3+ |  4+ |  3  |  1  |                   | 15     |

#### Configuration

An ODST Combat Team consists of 3-5 ODSTs, one ODST may be replaced with an ODST Sergeant.

#### Equipment

An ODST is equipped with an M7 SMG and M9 Grenades.

Any ODST may replace its M7 SMG with any of the following:

- M6 Pistol
- M90 Shotgun
- BR55 Service Rifle
- MA5 Assault Rifle

One ODST may replace its M7 SMG with one of the following: M41 Rocket Launcher or SRS99 Sniper Rifle.
